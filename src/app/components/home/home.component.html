Value Created

Without Viva Music

Year Activity Result Value
1 Instrument is finished being used and is now sitting in a room. Nothing. $0
2 Instrument is found during spring cleaning and is moved to attic. Nothing. $0
3 Space is needed in the attic, so instrument is moved to basement. Nothing. $0
10 Instrument is still sitting in basement, untouched. Kids still can't play music, and you'll never know what your instrument could have done. $0
You forget your instrument even existed; meanwhile, you question your helplessness as many are denied the opportunity to learn music.

With Viva Music

Year Activity Result Value
1 Instrument is no longer being used, so it is donated to Viva Music. In the first year, a student rents out the instrument. ~$300
2 The instrument is rented out for the second year. A kid who uses needs it can use it. ~$600
3 For the third year in a row, your instrument is rented out. You go onto the follow tab and see the impact ~$900
10 For the 10th year, your instrument is rented out. You see on the follow tab and see the value your instrument created. $3000
You feel happy and good knowing that you could help someone while creating ~$3000 worth of value for your instrument.
